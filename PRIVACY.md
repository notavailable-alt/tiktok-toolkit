# Privacy Policy for tiktok-toolkit

**Last Updated: February 22, 2026**

At **tiktok-toolkit**, we prioritize the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by tiktok-toolkit and how we use it.

## 1. Information We Collect
To provide our services, we use the TikTok Login Kit. When you authenticate with TikTok, we may access:
* **Basic Profile Information:** Open ID, display name, and profile avatar.
* **Public Metadata:** Information you have chosen to make public on your TikTok profile.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information collected solely for:
* Providing and maintaining our service.
* Identifying you within the app dashboard.
* Enhancing user experience.

**Note:** We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.

## 3. Data Retention
We only retain collected information for as long as necessary to provide you with your requested service. You can revoke our access at any time through your TikTok account settings.
